Hollow Knight is a classically styled 2D action adventure across a vast interconnected world. Explore twisting caverns, ancient cities and deadly wastes; battle tainted creatures and befriend bizarre bugs; and solve ancient mysteries at the kingdom's heart.

Features

Brave the Depths of a Forgotten Kingdom

Beneath the fading town of Dirtmouth sleeps a vast, ancient kingdom. Many are drawn beneath the surface, searching for riches, or glory, or answers to old secrets. As the enigmatic Knight, you’ll traverse the depths, unravel its mysteries and conquer its evils.

Use Your Skills and Reflexes to Survive

Hollow Knight is a challenging 2D action-adventure. You’ll explore twisting caverns, battle tainted creatures and escape intricate traps, all to solve an ancient long-hidden mystery.

Evocative Hand-Drawn Art

The world of Hollow Knight is brought to life in vivid, moody detail, its caverns alive with bizarre and terrifying creatures, each animated by hand in a traditional 2D style. Every new area you’ll discover is beautifully unique and strange, teeming with new creatures and characters to discover. The world of Hollow Knight is one worth exploring just to take in the sights and discover new wonders hidden off of the beaten path.

Characters

The game features around 50 to 60 NPCs, including friendly characters, shopkeepers, and quest-givers. These NPCs provide lore, assistance, and other interactions throughout the game. There are 47 bosses in the base game, and this number increases with the inclusion of the DLCs. With all content considered, the game has a total of 47 bosses.

Here are some of the most notable characters in Hollow Knight.

The Knight

The Knight is the protagonist and the only playable character of Hollow Knight. They are the child of the Pale King and the White Lady, born in the Abyss with Void inside their shell.

Hornet

Hornet is the mysterious princess-protector of Hallownest’s ruins, one of the bosses in Hollow Knight and the protagonist of Hollow Knight: Silksong.

The Hollow Knight

The Hollow Knight is the Vessel chosen by the Pale King to save Hallownest from the Infection. The Hollow Knight is one of the main bosses in Hollow Knight and they are central to the Knight's journey and the story of Hallownest.

The Radiance

The Radiance is a higher being of light similar to Essence, and as such, opposed to the Void, her ancient enemy. With the Infection, the Radiance offered unity to bugs at the cost of a mind incapable of thought. She is the final boss of Hollow Knight.

Nightmare King Grimm

Grimm is the master of the Grimm Troupe, a mysterious travelling circus. Grimm and his Troupe travel from the Nightmare Realm to wherever the Nightmare Lantern has been lit by acolytes. Nightmare King Grimm is the optional final boss of The Grimm Troupe.

Mantis Lords

The Mantis Lords are three sisters who lead the Mantis Tribe deep within the Fungal Wastes. They managed to resist the Infection throughout Hallownest. Mantis Lords remain proud warriors, and they grant passage into Deepnest to any who would defeat them in a fair challenge.

The Pale King

The Pale King is a higher being who used to be the monarch of Hallownest, mate to the White Lady, and ruler of the White Palace. The Pale King was often depicted as an imposing, gleaming figure with a fiercely horned crown. Although he was an elusive, reclusive figure who was rarely seen outside his Palace, the Pale King was deified by his citizens.

The White Lady

The White Lady is a root higher being who used to be the Queen of Hallownest and the wife of the Pale King. The White Lady is the mother of the Vessels.

The Hunter

When the Hunter was young, he and his siblings used to hunt each other in their nest. Ever since then, he hunts alone without any companion or partner. He looks down on the bugs of the kingdom of Hallownest, regarding them as weak and pathetic, with their kingdom being destined for ruin.

Cornifer

When Cornifer was first hatched, he wandered off right away, leaving his siblings and his mother behind. He later moved to Dirtmouth with Iselda where he sells maps.

The Last Stag

The Last Stag is a large stag beetle and the last of his kind in Hallownest. Even though his own kind disappeared and old age starts impairing him, the Last Stag remains faithful to his duties. He transports any bug calling him with the bell of a Station, wherever they are.

Dirtmouth

Dirtmouth, the Fading Town, is the first town on the way to Hallownest, located in the valley between the Howling Cliffs and Crystal Peak. In Hollow Knight, it initially has only one resident, but more bugs can return or move into town depending on certain events.

Forgotten Crossroads

Located right below the surface, the Forgotten Crossroads consists of many caverns and roads. Fossilised remains of bugs cover the surfaces of the caverns, with pale ferns growing through cracks in the ground.

Greenpath

Greenpath is an exuberant green cavern, composed of diverse vegetation, mossy valleys, and broad lakes of acid. These features are deeply reflected in its inhabitants, who developed a natural mossy camouflage, allowing them to perfectly blend in with their surroundings.

Crystal Peak

Crystal Peak contains extensive tunnels throughout the mountain which were used to mine its crystals. The crystals are present across the entire area, with some tunnels having crystals lining the top and bottom of the cavern. Conveyor belts can be found in many rooms which transport the crystal shards.

City of Tears

The City of Tears is built in an enormous cavern below the Blue Lake. It has numerous large towers with expansive windows which offer views of the city. Going up and down these towers is possible via small lifts.

Resting Grounds

The Resting Grounds were a sacred place where bugs would come to conduct rituals for the dead. Tombstones and monuments cover the caverns of the Resting Grounds. The west part of the Resting Grounds contains the Blue Lake.

Fog Canyon

The misty caverns of Fog Canyon are filled with bubbles and acid lakes, with some areas covered in thorns. The ground and walls usually consist of rocks or short, green flora. The bubbles in the region have trapped swirling gases inside them, and the ones with an orange glow can explode when they are popped.

Fungal Wastes

The noxious caverns of the Fungal Wastes are covered in mushrooms of all kinds. Besides this, caustic pools of acid are abundant throughout the area. The native population of the Fungal Wastes mainly consists of the Mushroom Clan and the Mantis Tribe.

Kingdom's Edge

The cliff range of Kingdom's Edge is characterised by its strong winds and ash that blankets the caverns. There is little vegetation except for large white roots that protrude from the walls and colourless plants that grow on numerous surfaces. The rest of the cavern walls consist of rocks and fossilised shells.

Queen's Gardens

The Gardens are filled with overgrown vegetation, thorny vines, and neglected greenhouses. Lanterns with Lumaflies light up the area. Besides the Hallownest structures and flowering plants, tents have cropped up in the Gardens, housing the Mantis Traitors.

Deepnest

What most identifies Deepnest is its gloomy surroundings. Thick layers of web covering sometimes large portions of the background and twisted maze-like tunnels and passages, several having the dim glow of a bioluminescent fungus as the only source of light. Most parts of Deepnest are dark or pitch black and can be illuminated with the Lumafly Lantern.

Royal Waterways

Many areas in the Waterways are flooded by the water constantly dripping from above, originating from the rain in the City of Tears (and ultimately from the Blue Lake). Several rooms in the waterways are littered with piles of trash.

The Hive

Particular to the Hive are structures built out of brown and translucent honey-like materials. The architecture also follows a hexagonal shape reminiscent of honeycombs. The Hive is inhabited by a species of airborne warriors ruled by a queen. Though the colony exists within Hallownest, they remained separate from the kingdom's affairs.

Ancient Basin

An ancient area, Ancient Basin is filled with old structures and roads that were formed as if the rock itself possessed a will. Ancient Basin mainly consists of rocks and fossils with occasional roots scattered throughout the area. A Tram is located in the north part which leads to Deepnest and Kingdom's Edge.
